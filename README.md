# Olist E-Commerce Logistics & Performance Dashboard
**Target Audience:** Marketing Executinve Team and Growth Stakeholders
**Objective:** Optimize post-purchase customer experience and identify operational bottlenecks to protect marketing ROI and drive repeat sales.

---

## Executive Summary
While marketing effort have successfully driven customer acquisition,post-purchase operations heavily impact our brand reputation and customer lifetime value (LTV). This analysis bridges the gap between **logistics efficiency** and **customer satisfaction**.

By tracking fulfillment bottlenecks alongside revenue trends and review scores, this dashboard provides the marketing and growth teams with actionable insights on where our supply chain is thriving and where shipping delays threaten customer retention.

---

#### **Core Metrics Tracked:**
* **Average Delivery Days:** The baseline speed of our shipping pipeline.
* **Delivery Variance Days:** The gap between promised delivery dates and actual arrival.
* **Fulfillment Rate:** The percentage of orders successfully processed without disruption.

#### *Strategic Business Insights:**
* **The Business Problem:** Our **Regional Logistics Performance** and **Delivery Volatility** metrics show that the shipping consistency varies wildly by region. When the **Delivery Variance** spikes, customer expectations are broken.
* **The Recommendation (Action Grid):** Marketing should utilise the **Action Grid** data to identify regions with low volatility and high fulfillment rates. Target aggressive promotional spend *only* in these high efficiency zones to gurantee a premuim customer experience.

#### **Core Metrics Tracked:**
* **Total Revenue & Total Orders:** High-level commercial health and volume.
* **Total Customers:** Active buyer acquisition scale.
* **Average Review Score:** The ultimate pulse check on customer satisfaction.

#### **Strategic Business Insights:**
* **Sales Trends:** The **Count of Order By Month** chart highlights our seasonal demand peaks , allowing marketing to plan the budget around historical high-volume months.
* **Customer Preferences:** The **Count of Paymenents by Payment Type** breakdown reveals exactly how our customers prefer to transact, guiding promotional partnerships (e.g., offering discounts for the most utilized payment methods).
* **The Retention Risk:** By cross-referencing the **Count of Review Scores** graph against our average overall score, we can pinpoint whether negative reviews are spiking. This provides an early warning system: if satisfaction drops, marketing should pause brand-building campaigns and coordinate with operations to fix the root cause.

## Core Business Questions Answered in This Analysis
1. **How volatile is our shipping pipeline**, and regions pose the highest risk for delivery delays?
2. **Are we meeting our delivery promises**, or is a widening "Delivery Variance" hurting our brand reputation?
3. **What are our primary volume drivers** in terms of monthly order trends and prefered payment methods?
4. **How healthy is our customer satisfaction overall**, and what volume of orders is recieving poor review scores?

## Analytics Infrastructure (Behind The Scenes)
*For technical stakeholders interested in how these insights were engineered:*

* **Data Modeling:** Intregrated complex e-commerce transactinal data into an optimized Star Schema inside PowerBI using Power Query.
* **Advanced DAX:** Engineered custom metrics for Average Delivery Days, Delivery Variance, and time-based order aggregations.
* **Data Prep:** Pre-cleaned and filtered out noise from over 100k mixed-data rows using optimized SQL/Excel steps to insure instantaneous dashboard cross-filtering.
